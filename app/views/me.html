Where it All Began

I am the offspring of a jazz musician and an actor who gave me one piece of advice: Don't pursue a career in the creative arts. Being the obedient young person I was, I studied economics and finance at Melbourne University.

During this time, I discovered computer programming - the world's most powerful problem solving tool. It allowed me to express my creativity, bounded only by the edges of my imagination.

I now work as a designer and front end developer for a Melbourne-based innovation agency. In my spare time I'm continually exploring the applications of computer science in the creative arts, among other technology-focused projects of many forms.

I'm easily excitable and love chatting about interesting ideas. So feel free to get in touch to chat about anything and everything creative.
