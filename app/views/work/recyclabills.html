Recently I built a website and online store for Recyclabills - handmade wallets made from recycled Bonsoy containers.

I started making these wallets late last year, and people seemed to like the idea. So I thought I could give myself some practice integrating a payment gateway to my web-apps, and support Pollinate Energy while I was at it!

Check the site out at recyclabills.com, and you can even buy a wallet if you feel like it!
